Overview

The Stock Management System is a powerful web-based inventory management solution designed to help businesses track products, manage suppliers, control stock movements, and generate accurate reports.

The system provides real-time inventory visibility while supporting batch tracking, expiry date monitoring, and automated alerts to ensure efficient stock control and prevent losses.

Features
  • Product and category management
  • Real-time stock tracking
  • Supplier management
  • Purchase order management
  • Batch-wise inventory tracking
  • Expiry date monitoring and alerts
  • FEFO (First Expiry First Out) stock movement
  • Low stock and out-of-stock notifications
  • Sales integration with POS systems
  • Inventory and supplier performance reports
  • Role-based user access control
  • Activity logs and audit trails
